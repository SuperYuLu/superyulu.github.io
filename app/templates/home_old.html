Research

My research in focusing on an alternative cooling method for large BEC( Bose-Einstein Condensate ). We are achieving this through two parallel apparches: adiabatic deceleration of supersonic lithium atoms, Magneto-Optical Colling (MOP).

In the first apprach, I work on building a 2.5-meters-long magnetic decelerator, which consist of 480 anti-Helmholtz coils with micro-seconds-level time time sequence control by FPGA ( Field Programmable Gate Array ). Fast moving but inertially cold atoms are decelerated from about 500 m/s to rest while keep the atom cloud cold under the vacuum. High flux and mili-Kelvin atom source was generated.
