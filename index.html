INTRO

What I am all about

I was born in a small town in Northwest China, My childhood dream was to be a bus driver, as I grew up, this dream changed to being a taxi driver, then a physicist. Driven by this dream, I chose to study theoritical Physics and got my bachelor's degree from University of Science and Technology of China . Later I found my interest in experimental atomic and optical physics, so I started my Ph.D. in AMO physics in University of Texas at Austin .

While enjoying the joy of sloving physics problems in the lab, I'm also excited with programming , including physical simulation with C/C++/python, web front end design with HTML/CSS, backend development using python and PostgreSQL, parallel computing with C++/Fortran, IoT device programming using python / linux shell script, Labview experiment cotrol and DAQ, as well as statistical learning and data science, etc. I'm also a big fan of linux OS.
